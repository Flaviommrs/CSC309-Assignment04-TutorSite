Welcome to Tutor Me!

Struggling in a course? Need to get that A+ average? Tutor Me is here to help! Tutor Me is a website that provides tutoring services for students who need knowledgable teachers, but don't want to break the bank.

Aimed towards highschool students, Tutor Me is a peer-to-peer tutoring service. Students who need help can find local tutors in their area, and students who excel academically can offer their skills for a fee. We offer tutors in common highschool subjects, such as calculus and vectors, physics, history and many more. Our website makes it easy for students to meet and schedule appointments with our tutors, as well as hire multiple tutors for various subjects.

Our goal is to create a network of peers who's aim is to improve their educational understanding at an affordable rate. Those who need help can find it without overspending, and those who want to offer their services can be awarded for their understanding while strengthening their knowledge.
